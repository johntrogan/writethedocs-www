# Venue

## Treasury Theatre

Lower Plaza, 1 Macarthur St, Melbourne

Our conference will be held at [Treasury Theatre](https://www.dtf.vic.gov.au/treasury-theatre-bookings), a modern theatre-style auditorium.

Established as the State Film Centre in the  1960s, the theatre underwent an architect-led  refurbishment in 2015. Its state-of-the-art  audio visual systems and elegant heritage features  are sure to impress. 

The venue is conveniently located in central  Melbourne, near Treasury Gardens, on the corner of Spring Street and Macarthur Street in East Melbourne.

## Getting there

The Treasury Theatre is easily accessible by public transport. The venue is centrally located and well serviced by Melbourne's public transport system, including buses, trams, and a train station right opposite the venue.
